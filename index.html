Amir Temur is a Central Asian Turkic ruler, general and officer who played an important role in the history of Central, South and Western Asia, as well as the Caucasus, Volga and Russia. Sarkarda was the founder of the Timurid kingdom (1370) with Samarkand as its capital. Amir Temur was born on April 9, 1336 in the village of Khoja Ilgor (Yakkabog) near Kesh (Shahrisabz).His father, Amir Muhammad Taraghai, was one of the barlos of the Turkish generation, and he was one of the influential intellectuals in Movarounnahr. In 1356, 2 sons of Amir Temur were born - Jahangir and Omar Shaykh.Amir Temur died in the city of Otrar in 1405 during his march to China.
